FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to replace my TMS?

No. Oviq sits above your existing TMS. Keep using McLeod, Aljex, AscendTMS, Rose Rocket, or whatever you have. Setup takes under an hour — schedule one report, set one forwarding rule, and you're live.

What counts as a load?

A load is any shipment record imported into Oviq in a given month. Loads that don't generate exceptions still count toward your volume.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes, at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the start of your next billing period.

What happens if I go over my limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade before exceeding it, or we'll apply a per-load overage rate.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a guided demo and a 14-day pilot for qualified freight brokerages. Book a demo to get started.

How does billing work?

Monthly, billed on your signup date. Annual billing with a discount is available on Growth and Professional — contact us.

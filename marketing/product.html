Workflow

From detection to resolution
in minutes, not hours.

Four steps. Most handled automatically.

01

Detect

Oviq continuously evaluates every shipment against expected pickup, delivery, and status milestones. Exceptions surface the moment data changes.

02

Case opened

Every exception becomes a structured case with priority, timeline, assigned playbook, and full audit trail — created and tracked automatically.

03

AI resolves

Oviq drafts and sends carrier and customer communications, requests ETAs, creates follow-ups, and logs every action with a confidence score.

04

Escalate when needed

When confidence falls below threshold, carriers go dark, or financial exposure crosses a limit — Oviq escalates with full context pre-loaded.

Resolution playbooks

Define the rules once.
Oviq runs them every time.

Each exception type has a configurable, step-by-step playbook. AI steps execute automatically; human steps create tasks.

Missed Pickup
Contact carrier→Notify customer→Follow up in 60 min→Escalate if no response
Late Delivery
Request ETA→Notify customer→Escalate if SLA missed
Missing POD
Request POD from carrier→Follow up at 24 hrs→Escalate at 48 hrs

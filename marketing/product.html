Product

Exception operations,
fully automated.

Oviq is an AI-native operations layer that sits above your TMS — detecting every shipment exception, running resolution playbooks, and escalating only when human judgment is genuinely required.

Workflow

From detection to resolution
in minutes, not hours.

Four steps. Most handled automatically.

01

Detect

Oviq continuously evaluates every shipment against expected pickup, delivery, and status milestones. Exceptions are identified the moment data changes.

02

Case opened

Every exception becomes a structured Case with priority, timeline, assigned playbook, and full audit trail — automatically created and tracked.

03

AI resolves

Oviq drafts and sends carrier and customer communications, requests ETAs, creates follow-up tasks, and logs every action with a confidence score.

04

Escalate only when needed

When AI confidence falls below threshold, carriers go dark, or financial exposure crosses a limit — Oviq escalates with full context pre-loaded.

Oviq is not a TMS replacement — it's an operations layer that sits above your existing stack. No API integration required. No IT project. Two steps and you're live.

Scheduled TMS report

Every TMS can schedule a recurring shipment report and email it automatically. Point that report at your unique Oviq ingest address — Oviq learns your column format on the first file and parses every subsequent report instantly.
